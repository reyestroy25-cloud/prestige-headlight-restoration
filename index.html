FAQ

How long does headlight restoration take?

Most vehicles take about 45 to 90 minutes depending on the condition of the headlights.

Can all headlights be restored?

Most cloudy, yellow, or oxidized plastic headlights can be improved. If the lens is cracked, damaged inside, or severely burned, replacement may be needed.

How long will it last?

Results depend on sun exposure, vehicle storage, and the protection used. UV protection helps slow future yellowing.

Do you come to my location?

Yes, mobile service is available in Ventura County. Text your city and vehicle for availability.

What is the best package?

The Premium Restoration is the best choice for most vehicles because it includes multi-stage sanding, polishing, and UV protection.
